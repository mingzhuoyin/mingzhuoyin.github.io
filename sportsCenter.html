FIND THE CENTER

Dressing Room

Our spacious dressing room offers ample storage facilities and comfortable changing areas for our players. Here, you can find all the necessary sports gear and conveniently change your clothes, getting fully prepared for the game or training.

Steam Room

Our steam room offers a relaxing sanctuary for our players. Here, you can open up your pores, eliminate toxins, and improve blood circulation through steam baths. It's an excellent way to unwind and rejuvenate your body after a hard day's work on the court.

Restaurant

Our restaurant offers a diverse range of delicious cuisine, including healthy meals, tasty snacks, and indulgent desserts. Here, you can enjoy a satisfying meal while replenishing your energy for the next basketball session.

Shop

Our shop stocks a wide array of basketball-related merchandise, including jerseys, sneakers, balls, and protective gear. Whether you need new sports equipment or are looking for unique basketball souvenirs, you'll find it all here. Additionally, we offer customization services to create exclusive basketball gear tailored to your preferences.

Massage Room

After an intense game or training session, our massage room provides the perfect relaxation. Skilled masseurs offer rejuvenating massages to soothe muscle fatigue, helping you recover quickly and get ready for your next challenge.

Gym

Equipped with state-of-the-art fitness equipment and professional trainers, our gym caters to all your fitness needs. Whether you want to build muscle strength or enhance flexibility, this is the perfect place for you to take your physical fitness to the next level and prepare better for basketball matches.
